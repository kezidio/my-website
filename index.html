About me

Kaweny is a results-driven professional seeking to expand her expertise in backend development and software engineering. In her current role, she contributes to advancing digital engagement through the development and maintenance of web solutions using WordPress and application development tools. With a strong background in web development and customer engagement, she brings excellent communication skills and a user-centered mindset to her work.

Previously, as a Sales Consultant at Legisweb, she traveled around Brazil speaking at business conferences to present the company’s application to stakeholders and support business operations. This role involved regularly reaching out to customers to buil and maintain professional relationships.

In addition, Kaweny is a multilingual professional, fluent in English and a native Portuguese speaker. She worked in a Cultural Exchange Program and earned a degree in Computer Science in the United States. During the program, she improved group and individual performance by applying positive management strategies. Her role involved promoting emotional, cognitive, and creative development through structured and interactive activities.

Consistently aiming to deliver her best work, Kaweny builds strong relationships by understanding others’ needs and striving for win-win solutions. She is pragmatic and open-minded in her approach to problem-solving, and remains focused on her objectives while considering different perspectives.
